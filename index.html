Amazon's HQ2:

Did Raleigh really stand a chance?

By Rachel McKinney

On September, 7th 2017, Amazon announced to the world that they would be opening a second headquarters, kicking off a massive competition between municipalities to win over Amazon and the 50,000 high-paying jobs their headquarters would bring. Raleigh, NC managed to make the top 20 list of locations for Amazon's new HQ2 location out of the 236 cities vying to become Amazon's home. On November 13th 2018, Amazon announced it's new HQ2 would be split between Arlington, VA and Long Island City, NYC. So how did Raleigh stack up against these cities? Let's take a look at what Amazon was looking for from municipalities.

Amazon's HQ2 Wish List:

  1. 30 miles to population centers
  2. 45 minutes to an international airport
  3. No more than 1-2 miles from major highways
  4. Direct access to public transit
  5. Tax incentives
  6. Educated labor pool and strong university System
  7. An overall high quality of life
